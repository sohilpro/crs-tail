About

In 2020, CrsTrip was founded with the aim of providing online airline ticket sales services to destinations worldwide. This company, with an experienced and specialized team, managed to become one of the most reputable airline ticket sales companies in Iran in a short period of time. CRSTrip, in collaboration with over 100 reputable airlines worldwide, makes it easy and quick for its customers to purchase airplane tickets. Customers of this company can buy their plane tickets to their desired destinations at any time, day or night, from anywhere, using the CRSTrip website or app.
